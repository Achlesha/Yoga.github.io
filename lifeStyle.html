5 Healthy Life Hacks

1. Start Your Day With H2O

  • Start your morning off right with a big glass of water.
    • After fasting all night, this first drink of water will help to hydrate your cells and wake you up.
    • Can boost metabolism.
    • Better skin health.
    • Boost energy levels.

2. Prioritize Your Sleep

  • The Benefits of Getting a Full Night's Sleep
    • Sleep Can Boost Your Immune System.
    • Sleep Can Strengthen Your Heart
    • Better Sleep = Better Mood

3. Practice Self Care

  • No matter how busy your life gets, make sure you prioritize yourself and your health and practice self-care
    • Creates a Healthy Relationship with yourself
    • Boosts Physical and Mental Well-Being
    • Feel a great sense of fulfillment

4. Switch to a standing desk.

  • Sitting at your desk for eight hours (or more) per day puts recurring pressure
    on the base of your spine, leading to long-term issues, like chronic back pain
    • You can switch to an adjustable standing desk and shoot to stand for at least half of your workday
    • And if you're a regular sufferer of chronic back pain , contact with your doctor

5. Look into the distance.

  • When you look near (like you are right now), the ciliary muscles in your eyes contract and stay that way.
    • When you look into the distance, however, the ciliary muscles relax.
    • So, for every 20 minutes make a habit of taking a 20-second break to look as far into the distance as you can.
